The mark
A faceted shield that reads as a G — protection carved in origami planes, holding a seal-square at its core: the hanko of a signed, verifiable record.
Clear space
Keep space equal to the height of the seal-square clear on every side. Never crowd the mark with type or edges.
Minimums & don'ts
Mark no smaller than 24px. Don't recolor the seal, rotate, add effects, or place the light lockup on busy or mid-tone backgrounds.
