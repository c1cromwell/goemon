Mission & framing

Mission: Operate Argus Financial Partners with the smallest possible human staff — an auditable, agent-native corporation where every material decision is logged and the CEO retains final authority on money, launches, and legal posture.

Phase A framing: Tokenization-first, non-custodial, agentic finance. Keys on-device; agents under scoped credentials; append-only audit. Not a bank — the all-seeing guardian of your money.
