Goemon logo concepts

Subtle reference to 剣に生き剣に死す — “live by the sword, die by the sword.” For Goemon: we honor every decision to our customers, or we cease to exist. Kanji variants: 剣 (sword), 誠 (sincerity/honor). Pick a direction for the primary wordmark; we refine one SVG for production.

Eye over crossed blades; bushido without clutter.

02 — 剣 watermark

Single kanji “sword” ghosted behind the eye.

03 — 誠 honor

Sincerity kanji; “honor the word” framing.

04 — Twin pillars

Parallel swords frame the sentinel eye.

05 — Ninja sentinel

Standing silhouette; eye as chest mark.

06 — Hanko seal

Circular stamp; 剣に生き arc at base.

07 — Proverb arc

Full phrase as subtle arc above the eye.
