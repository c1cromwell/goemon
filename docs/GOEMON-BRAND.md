# Goemon Global Finance — Brand Guide (R1)

**Legal:** Goemon Global Finance, LLC (Wyoming) · **DBA / product:** Goemon · **Domain:** goemonglobal.com

This document is the canonical brand narrative for docs, product copy, and counsel review. Logo direction and public folklore marketing are **deferred** (see `docs/GOEMON-REBRAND-PLAN.md`).

---

## 1. Who we are

Goemon Global Finance is a **tokenization-first** financial platform: non-custodial wallet, double-entry ledger, Hedera settlement, agent-native commerce (W3C Verifiable Credentials + MCP), and tokenized real-world assets and collectibles — built so customers keep control of keys while software enforces compliance and audit.

We are **not a bank** in Phase A. We build software and rails; regulated partners move fiat when required.

---

## 2. Folklore anchor (internal / culture — public use deferred)

**Ishikawa Goemon** (石川 五右衛門) — in folklore, a figure who took from the powerful and stood with the oppressed; historically likely an Iga-trained operative turned outlaw. For Goemon this is **product truth**, not costume:

| Theme | Product expression |
|---|---|
| Resist extractive intermediaries | Zero-interchange native rail, transparent fees, agent-scoped access |
| Protect the vulnerable | Non-custodial keys, VP-gated agents, fraud freeze, escrow |
| Discipline | Balanced ledger, append-only audit, human gates on money and legal |

Do **not** use exploitative ninja caricature in regulated copy. Prefer abstract marks (誠, discipline, balance).

---

## 3. Motto (customer-facing)

> **Build | Protect | Preserve** — *Your Trust & Your Assets*

| Pillar | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Build** | Wealth tools: tokenized assets, savings, agent commerce, marketplace |
| **Protect** | Keys, scopes, fraud, reconciliation — security as product |
| **Preserve** | Ledger truth, audit trail, human CEO/legal gates |

## 5. Voice & tone

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Precise, calm, institutional where money/legal | Hype, “moon”, gambling language |
| “Goemon” or “Goemon Global Finance” in legal/footer | Call ourselves a “bank” without charter |
| Explain agent permissions in plain language | Imply agents can act without user grant |
| Integer money, source/as-of on quotes | Floats, hidden fees |

**Quiet Premium** design system (Phase 9): monochrome surfaces, single jade accent `#2dd4a7`, type-led hierarchy, dark default.
