**ORGANIZATIONAL ACTION BY UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT**
**OF THE ORGANIZER, INITIAL MANAGER, AND MEMBERS OF**
**GOEMON GLOBAL FINANCE, LLC**
**(A Wyoming Limited Liability Company)**

**Effective Date:** `[• date]`

The undersigned, being the **organizer**, the **initial Manager**, and **all of the Members**
of Goemon Global Finance, LLC (the "**Company**"), acting by unanimous written consent in lieu
of an organizational meeting pursuant to the Wyoming LLC Act and the Company's Operating
Agreement, hereby adopt the following resolutions, which shall have the same force as if
adopted at a duly called and held meeting.

**RESOLVED**, that the **Articles of Organization** of the Company, filed with the Wyoming
Secretary of State on `[• filing date]` (filing ID `[• ]`), are in all respects **ratified,

### 2. Adoption of the Operating Agreement

**RESOLVED**, that the **Operating Agreement** of the Company (`OPERATING-AGREEMENT.md`), in
the form presented to and reviewed by the Members, is **adopted and approved** as the governing
operating agreement of the Company, and each Member is directed to execute it.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the Company is **manager-managed**, and the Members confirm that, as
provided in the Operating Agreement, **all Member action requires the unanimous consent of the
Members** until changed in accordance with the Operating Agreement.

### 3. Admission of Members and issuance of Units

**RESOLVED**, that the Company is authorized to issue up to **10,000,000 Units**; and the
following persons are **admitted as Members** and the following Units are **issued** to them,
fully vested, in exchange for their respective capital contributions (cash and contributed
intellectual property) described in Schedule A of the Operating Agreement and in Resolution 6
below:

| Member | Units issued | Class | Percentage Interest (fully reserved basis) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Chad Cromwell** | **5,100,000** | Class A Voting | **51%** |
| **Oran Cromwell** | **4,000,000** | Class A Voting | **40%** |

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that **900,000 Units (9%)** are **reserved and held unissued** by the
Company for issuance solely as awards under the Equity Incentive Plan (Resolution 7); such
reserved Units are not outstanding, do not vote, and receive no allocations or distributions
until issued as awards.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the Units issued to Chad Cromwell and Oran Cromwell are **fully
vested** and not subject to repurchase for failure to vest, and that the Manager shall record
the foregoing on the Company's cap table (Schedule A).

### 5. Tax matters and Partnership Representative

**RESOLVED**, that the Company shall be treated as a **partnership** for U.S. federal income
tax purposes (multi-member LLC), and no officer shall make an election to the contrary without
Member approval as a Reserved Matter.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that **Chad Cromwell** is designated the Company's **Partnership
Representative** under IRC § 6223 (BBA centralized partnership audit rules).

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the fiscal year of the Company is the **calendar year**, subject to
change on CPA advice. **⚖**

**RESOLVED**, that the appropriate officer is authorized and directed to apply for an

### 7. Adoption of the Equity Incentive Plan

**RESOLVED**, that the **Goemon Global Finance, LLC Equity Incentive Plan (Unit Option &
Profits Interest Plan)** (`EQUITY-INCENTIVE-PLAN.md`) is **adopted and approved**, and that
**900,000 Units (9%)** are reserved for issuance thereunder.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the **Manager is authorized to grant profits interests and Unit

venture/strategic partners, and to execute award agreements and joinders, without further
