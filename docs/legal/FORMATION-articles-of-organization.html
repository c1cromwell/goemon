Article V — Purpose

The Company is organized to engage in any lawful business, purpose, or activity for which a limited liability company may be organized under the laws of the State of Wyoming.

For descriptive reference (not a limitation): the Company develops and licenses non-custodial financial software and tokenization infrastructure — a non-custodial wallet, double-entry ledger, decentralized-identity/verifiable-credential systems, and agent-native commerce tooling. In Phase A the Company does not take custody of customer funds, does not move customer money as an intermediary, and is not a bank. See CORPORATE-STRUCTURE.md §6 and docs/GOEMON-BRAND.md §5. ⚖ regulated-scope counsel.

Article VI — Duration

The duration of the Company is perpetual, unless dissolved earlier in accordance with the Operating Agreement or the Wyoming LLC Act.

Article VII — Liability and Indemnification

No member or manager of the Company is personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company solely by reason of being a member or manager, as provided in Wyo. Stat. § 17-29-304. The Company shall indemnify its managers, members, and officers to the fullest extent permitted by the Wyoming LLC Act, as further set forth in the Operating Agreement (Article XII).
